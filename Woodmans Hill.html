Ensuring each child reaches their potential

Woodmans Hill is a standalone 7-12 secondary college

Woodmans Hill is set in well maintained, park like grounds at the entrance to Ballarat. We want Woodmans Hill to be your school of choice and encourage you to visit and talk to our staff, parents and current students to assist in making your decision.

Following extended consultation with our school community we are excited to introduce Woodmans Hill as a standalone 7-12 college from the beginning of 2019. Our school community has overwhelmingly asked for a learning environment where students can start and finish their secondary education, where relationships can be built and maintained and staff, students and parents know each other from start to finish because of the size of the school.
